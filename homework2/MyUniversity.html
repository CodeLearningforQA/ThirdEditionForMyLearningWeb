ABOUT MY SCHOOL

FPT University is a private university in Viet Nam, and founded by FPT Corporation - one of the country's top technology companies. Known for its strong focus on IT, business, and innovation, the university offers a dynamic and modern learning environment. Not only those, FPTU create opportunities for students to participate in exchange programs with universities abroad, as well.
